Caveats are modifiers or special handling instructions that describe how
information should be handled and to who it can be released or shared.
These markings are controlled by various instructions and documents
including (but not limited to): Executive Order 13292, Classified National
Security Information Directive No. 1, DoD Regulation 5200.1 and specific
program classification guides. Some common examples are listed below.

NATO UNCLASSIFIED
NATO CONFIDENTIAL
NATO RESTRICTED
ALLIED RESTRICTED
USA, _________ EYES ONLY
REL TO (AUTHORIZED FOR RELEASE TO) USA, ________________
